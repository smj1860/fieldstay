# Installing the Crew App

The FieldStay Crew App puts turnover checklists, inventory counts, and work orders
in your crew's hands — and works even without cell service or WiFi.

## Getting Access
Invite crew from Settings → Crew → Invite Crew Member. Share the crew app link:
https://app.fieldstay.app/crew

## Installing on iPhone (Safari Required)
Must use Safari — not Chrome.
1. Open Safari and go to app.fieldstay.app/crew
2. Log in with crew member's account
3. Tap the Share button (box with arrow pointing up)
4. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
5. Tap Add in the top right

## Installing on Android (Chrome)
1. Open Chrome and go to app.fieldstay.app/crew
2. Log in with crew member's account
3. Tap the three-dot menu → Add to Home Screen or Install App
4. Tap Install

## Notifications
Allow notifications when prompted. This alerts crew the moment a turnover or
work order is assigned without needing to open the app.

## Offline Mode
The app works without internet. Checklists, inventory counts, and photos all
function normally with no signal. Everything syncs automatically when connectivity
is restored — no action needed from the crew member.

## Troubleshooting
- Crew doesn't see turnovers: check that they are assigned to properties or
  turnovers in the dashboard; assignments sync within a few minutes
- App not updating: close and reopen the app, or pull down to refresh
- Install option not appearing on iPhone: must use Safari, not Chrome

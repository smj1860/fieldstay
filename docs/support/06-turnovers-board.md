# Understanding Your Turnovers Board

**The Turnovers Board is your real-time view of every property preparation window — what needs to be done, who's doing it, and when.**

---

## What Is a Turnover

A turnover is the preparation period between a guest checking out and the next guest checking in. FieldStay creates one automatically every time a new booking is confirmed in your connected PMS (OwnerRez or Hospitable), using the checkout and check-in dates to calculate how much time your crew has to prepare the property.

---

## The Board Layout

The Turnovers Board shows a card for each upcoming turnover. The default view shows the next 14 days so you open to an active, useful board — not a blank page.

Each card shows:
- **Property name** and the preparation window (checkout time → next check-in)
- **Status badge** — Pending, Assigned, In Progress, or Complete
- **Assigned crew member** if someone is assigned
- **Same-day flag** if checkout and check-in are on the same day

---

## Status Progression

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Pending | Turnover created, no crew assigned |
| Assigned | Crew member assigned, work not yet started |
| In Progress | Crew member tapped Start on their app |
| Complete | Crew member marked the turnover complete |

---

## Same-Day Turnovers

When a guest checks out and a new guest checks in on the same day, FieldStay flags the turnover with a same-day indicator. These require extra attention — the preparation window is tighter and any crew assignment delay cascades directly into the guest experience.

Same-day turnovers also trigger immediate inventory restock orders rather than waiting for the end-of-day summary.

---

## Assigning Crew

Click any turnover card to open the detail view. From there you can:

- **Assign a crew member** — select from your active crew list
- **Bulk assign** — select multiple turnovers and assign crew in one action

Once assigned, the turnover appears in the crew member's app immediately.

---

## Archiving Completed Turnovers

Completed turnovers stay on the board by default. To keep the board clean, click the menu on any completed card and select **Archive**. Archived turnovers are removed from the default view but remain accessible by toggling **Show Archived** above the board.

---

## Filtering the Board

Use the date filter to view a specific window. The default is the next 14 days. You can filter by property, crew member, or status to narrow the view.

# Setting Up Sponsors for Your Guidebook

**Sponsors are local businesses featured in your guest guidebook — they pay $15/month for placement and you earn plan credits that can make your FieldStay subscription free.**

---

## How the Sponsor Model Works

Each sponsor occupies a slot in your guidebook. Guests browsing the guidebook see the sponsor's offer — a discount, a free item, or a custom message — alongside a "just show this screen" prompt they can use at the business.

If you have opted-in SMS guests, sponsors in the right time slot may also be mentioned in the morning or evening recommendation message sent during the guest's stay.

**Sponsor thresholds:**
- 3 active sponsors → the Guidebook feature itself is permanently unlocked (free), separate from the rest of your FieldStay plan
- 5 active sponsors → $10/month credit applied to your next bill
- 6 active sponsors → $25/month credit applied to your next bill (does not stack with the 5-sponsor credit)

---

## Slot Types

Choose the slot type when setting up a sponsor. This controls when and how they appear:

| Slot | When It Fires |
|---|---|
| Morning Brew | Morning recommendation (coffee shops, breakfast spots) |
| Dinner & Pints | Evening recommendation (restaurants, bars) |
| Rainy Day | When precipitation is 60%+ (indoor activities, delivery) |
| Outdoor Adventure | Activity outfitters, bike rentals, kayak tours |
| General | Any recommendation context |

## Approaching a Local Business

The pitch is simple: *"I manage vacation rentals in the area. My guests ask me for local recommendations constantly. For $15/month you get featured in their digital guidebook and I'll recommend you in the text messages I send during their stay."*

Most independent coffee shops, restaurants, and activity outfitters understand word-of-mouth from property managers immediately. You're not cold-calling strangers — you're already a local operator they want to be connected to.

The media kit link (see below) gives them a professional preview before committing.

---

## Adding a Sponsor

Go to **Guidebook → Sponsors → Add Sponsor** and fill in:

**Business details:**
- Business name and short description (shown to guests)
- Phone number and website (optional, shown to guests)
- Business address (used for proximity matching to the property)

**Offer:**
- Offer type: percentage off, dollar amount off, free item, or custom text
- Offer value and item description
- Custom offer text if applicable

**Slot:**
- Select the slot type that best matches the business
- You can add context notes visible only to you

After saving, the sponsor enters **Pending** status until their first successful payment. Once payment clears they move to **Active** and appear in the guidebook.

---

## The Media Kit Link

Every sponsor has an auto-generated media kit link. Send it to the business owner before or after your pitch — it shows them exactly what their placement looks like in the guidebook, complete with the offer display and your property branding.

Find the link under **Guidebook → Sponsors → [Sponsor Name] → Share Media Kit**.

---

## Sponsor Billing

Sponsors are billed monthly via Stripe. FieldStay handles the billing relationship directly — you don't collect payments or issue invoices. If a sponsor's payment fails, they enter a grace period before being deactivated. You'll be notified so you can follow up with them.

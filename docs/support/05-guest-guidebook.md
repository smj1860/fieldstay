# How the Guest Guidebook Works

The FieldStay Guest Guidebook delivers your guests their door code, WiFi password,
and local recommendations via a personalized link — and turns local business
relationships into a revenue stream.

## What the Guest Experiences
Every guest gets a pre-arrival email with a link to their personalized guidebook:
door access code, WiFi name and password, check-in instructions, house rules,
and local recommendations from sponsored businesses.

## SMS Opt-In
The pre-arrival email prompts guests to enter their phone number for a door code
text message. Opted-in guests receive one check-in SMS with door code, WiFi, and
portal link. During the stay they may receive one morning and one evening
recommendation message if sponsors are configured. All messages include STOP.

## Setting Up the Guidebook
After OwnerRez sync, each property gets a guidebook pre-populated with WiFi and
check-in instructions. Review at Guidebook → [Property Name] and toggle Published
when ready. Guests cannot see it until it is published.

## The Sponsor Model
Local businesses pay $15/month per featured slot.
- 3 active sponsors: the Guidebook feature itself is permanently unlocked (free)
- 5 active sponsors: $10/month plan credit
- 6 active sponsors: $25/month plan credit

Go to Guidebook → Sponsors → Add Sponsor to add a business. A media kit link is
auto-generated to share with potential sponsors.

## Gap Night Messaging
When a guest is near checkout and there are open nights after their booking,
FieldStay can send an offer to extend their stay. Configure under Guidebook →
Gap Night Settings. Off by default.

## Contextual SMS Recommendations
Morning and evening messages are driven by property amenities from OwnerRez
(hot tub, fire pit, kayaks) and live weather data. A guest at a fire pit property
on a cool evening might receive a dinner recommendation from a local sponsor.

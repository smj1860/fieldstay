# About FieldStay

## What FieldStay Is

FieldStay has one primary mission: to make property managers' workday less stressful by helping them manage their operations.

FieldStay is a property operations platform built for short-term rental property managers who manage between 1 and 100 properties. It handles the work that happens between guest checkouts and check-ins — crew scheduling, turnover checklists, inventory tracking, maintenance work orders, vendor coordination, owner financials, and guest communications.

## Who Built It and Why

FieldStay was built by someone who has worked both sides of the operation.

I have worked in the field — cleaning, restocking, and counting inventory. I've also been the person in the office — scheduling crew, chasing them down for updates, and trying to figure out why a maintenance request fell through the cracks.

I know what both of those roles look like. That's why I built FieldStay.

That's why vendors don't have to create an account to read or complete a work order — because in the real world, they won't. They'll just ignore it.

That's why the checklist and inventory count system for crew is simple and straightforward — because the person restocking a property between turnovers doesn't need a complex app or the added stress of one. They need something that works fast, even with no cell signal, and gets out of their way.

That's why the scheduling system gets smarter the more turnovers you run through it — because operations shouldn't feel like groundhog day every single week.

## The Problem Nobody Talks About

There are hundreds of guest-facing SaaS platforms in the short-term rental industry. Rightly so — the guest experience matters.

But here's the truth nobody talks about: without clean properties, restocked supplies, and completed maintenance, all the guest-facing software in the world won't save your reviews.

The operations between turnovers are just as important. They just haven't had a platform built for them by someone who's actually done them. Until now.

# Hospitable Teammate Notifications vs. FieldStay Crew Assignments

If your cleaning crew or maintenance team is set up in Hospitable as teammates, they will receive two separate types of notifications when a new reservation comes in — one from Hospitable and one from FieldStay. These are different messages serving different purposes. This doc explains what each one is so you can set expectations with your crew.

---

## The Hospitable Notification (from Hospitable's system)

When a new reservation is confirmed in Hospitable, Hospitable automatically sends an email to any teammate whose role matches the service needed for that booking. For example, if a reservation requires cleaning, all teammates with the Cleaning role get an email.

**What it contains:**
- Property address
- Check-in and check-out date and time
- Number of guests
- A question asking if they're available

**What it is:** An availability check — Hospitable is asking if the crew member can do the job. It is not a confirmed assignment.

**Who sends it:** Hospitable, from their own system. It comes from a Hospitable email address, not from you or FieldStay.

**When it fires:** Immediately when the reservation is confirmed in Hospitable, before any turnover is created in FieldStay.

---

## The FieldStay Notification (from FieldStay's system)

FieldStay generates a separate notification when you assign a specific crew member to a turnover. This is the actual confirmed work assignment.

**What it contains:**
- The specific turnover they are assigned to (not just an availability check)
- Exact date, time window, and property
- A link to the FieldStay crew app where they can view the full checklist, access notes, lockbox code, and parking instructions
- SMS notification if they have a mobile number on file

**What it is:** A confirmed assignment — the crew member has been assigned to this specific turnover by the property manager.

**Who sends it:** FieldStay, from support@fieldstay.app or via SMS from the FieldStay number.

**When it fires:** When you assign a crew member to the turnover in your FieldStay dashboard. The turnover is generated automatically after the booking syncs from Hospitable.

---

## Summary: Which Is Which

| | Hospitable notification | FieldStay notification |
|---|---|---|
| **Sent by** | Hospitable | FieldStay |
| **Type** | Availability check | Confirmed assignment |
| **Contains** | Reservation details | Turnover details + crew app link |
| **Action required** | Reply to Hospitable | Open crew app and complete checklist |
| **Timing** | When reservation is confirmed | When PM assigns crew in FieldStay |

---

## What to Tell Your Crew

Let your crew members know they will receive both types of messages and what to do with each:

- **The Hospitable email** — this is Hospitable asking if you're available. You can respond to that separately.
- **The FieldStay message** — this is your confirmed assignment. When you receive this one, open the crew app link to see your checklist, access code, and full turnover details.

The FieldStay message is the one that matters for getting the job done. The Hospitable email is informational.

---

## Crew Members Who Were Synced From Hospitable

If you connected Hospitable to FieldStay and your crew members synced automatically, they are already in FieldStay as crew members. They will still receive the Hospitable availability emails from Hospitable's own system — FieldStay does not control those. Once you assign them to a turnover in FieldStay, they will also receive the FieldStay assignment notification.

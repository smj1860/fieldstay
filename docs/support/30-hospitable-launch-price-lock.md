# The Hospitable Launch Price Lock

FieldStay and Hospitable are launching an official integration partnership —
and to celebrate, the first 100 accounts that connect Hospitable and convert
to a paid FieldStay plan get their price locked for 2 years, automatically.

No code, no signup form, no extra step. If you're one of the first 100, you'll
see a Price Lock badge on your account settings page and get a confirmation
email the moment it happens.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What does "price locked" actually mean?

For the length of your lock, your plan's price won't go up — even if
FieldStay raises list prices for new customers during that time. You keep
paying what you were paying on the day you locked in, for as long as you stay
on that same tier.

### Do I need to do anything to get this?

No. If you connect Hospitable and you're one of the first 100 accounts to
convert from your free trial to a paid plan, it's applied automatically. You
don't need to enter a code, contact support, or opt in anywhere.

### How do I know if I got it?

You'll get an email from us the moment you're awarded. You'll also see a
prominently displayed Price Lock badge on your account settings page.

### I'm already a FieldStay customer. Can I get this by connecting Hospitable now?

No — this is for new trial signups only. It's tied to converting from a free
trial to a paid plan for the first time.

### What if I connect Hospitable but never convert to a paid plan?

The price lock only applies once you actually convert from your free trial to
a paid subscription. Connecting Hospitable alone doesn't reserve anything —
you're only awarded a lock once you become a paying customer.

### Does it matter if I connect through the Hospitable marketplace vs. connecting manually in FieldStay?

No — either way counts, as long as you're connected to Hospitable when you
convert to a paid plan.

### What if I disconnect Hospitable later? Do I lose the price lock?

No. Once you've been awarded a lock, it's yours for its full term regardless
of whether you keep Hospitable connected afterward.

### What happens if I upgrade to a bigger tier during my lock?

You'll pay that tier's current price going forward — the lock doesn't carry a
discount into a new tier. But your lock's clock keeps running, and you're
still protected from further price increases on your new tier for whatever
time is left.

### Does this apply to annual billing too?

Yes — whatever you're actually billed, monthly or annual, is what gets
locked in.

### What happens when my lock expires?

Your plan reverts to then-current pricing for your tier, the same as any
other customer.

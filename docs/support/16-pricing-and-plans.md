# FieldStay Pricing and Plans

FieldStay is priced by the number of properties you manage. Every plan includes the full platform — there are no feature gates or add-ons required to access core functionality.

---

## Plan Tiers

| Plan | Properties | Monthly Price |
|---|---|---|
| Starter | 1–15 | $199/month |
| Growth | 16–50 | $379/month |
| Portfolio | 51–100 | $599/month |
| Enterprise | 100+ | Custom pricing |

Contact support@fieldstay.app for Enterprise pricing.

---

## What Every Plan Includes

All plans include the complete FieldStay platform:

- Turnover management and crew scheduling
- Work orders and vendor management
- Inventory tracking and Kroger cart automation
- Asset health tracking and depreciation reporting
- Owner portal and financial ledger
- OwnerRez integration (Hostaway integration in progress)
- RepuGuard AI review response drafting
- Guest Guidebook with SMS delivery
- Crew mobile PWA (offline-capable)

There are no feature-gated tiers. Every PM on every plan has access to every feature.

---

## Annual Billing

Annual billing is available at a discount equivalent to 2 months free — roughly 17% off the monthly rate. Annual plans are billed once per year upfront.

To switch to annual billing, go to Settings → Billing or contact support@fieldstay.app.

---

## Guidebook Sponsor Credits

The Guest Guidebook includes a sponsor model where local businesses pay $15/month to be featured. Active sponsors generate plan credits that offset your FieldStay subscription:

- 4 active sponsors → your plan is free
- 5 active sponsors → $10/month credit applied to your next bill
- 6 active sponsors → $25/month credit applied to your next bill

Credits apply automatically each billing cycle based on your active sponsor count.

---

## Upgrading or Downgrading

Plans adjust automatically when your property count crosses a tier boundary. If you add properties that push you into the next tier, your plan upgrades at your next billing date. If you reduce properties below a tier threshold, your plan adjusts at the next billing date.

To manually change your plan, go to Settings → Billing → Change Plan, or contact support@fieldstay.app.

---

## Billing and Invoices

FieldStay billing is managed through Stripe. You can:

- View invoices at Settings → Billing
- Update your payment method at Settings → Billing → Payment Method
- Download past invoices from the billing portal

Invoices are sent to the email address on your FieldStay account on each billing date.

---

## Cancelling Your Subscription

To cancel, go to Settings → Billing → Cancel Plan, or email support@fieldstay.app. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation.

# Data Export and Account Deletion

What happens to your data when you cancel, how to request an export, and how account deletion works.

---

## Requesting a Data Export

If you'd like a copy of your FieldStay data — properties, bookings, turnovers, financial records, inventory history — email **support@fieldstay.app** with the subject line "Data Export Request." Exports are provided as CSV files and are typically delivered within 3 business days.

---

## What Happens When You Cancel Your Subscription

Cancelling your subscription stops billing but does not immediately delete your data. Go to **Settings → Billing → Cancel Plan** to cancel — your access continues through the end of your current billing period.

After cancellation:
- Your account becomes read-only at the end of the billing period
- All data (properties, turnovers, financial records, guidebook content) is retained for **30 days**
- You can reactivate your subscription at any time during the 30-day window with no data loss
- After 30 days, your data is permanently and irreversibly deleted

If you need an export, request it before the 30-day window closes.

---

## Deleting Your Account

Account deletion is a separate, more permanent action than cancelling a subscription. Deleting your account:

- Immediately cancels any active subscriptions (including Guidebook sponsor billing if applicable)
- Revokes all connected integrations (OwnerRez, Hostaway, Kroger)
- Removes your access to FieldStay
- Permanently deletes all associated data

**This action cannot be undone.** There is no 30-day grace period for account deletion — unlike subscription cancellation, deletion is immediate and final.

To delete your account, go to **Settings → Account → Delete Account**, or contact support@fieldstay.app if you'd prefer assistance with the process.

### Multi-Organization Accounts

If you belong to more than one organization in FieldStay, account deletion processes the cleanup for every organization you're a member of — subscriptions are cancelled and integrations revoked for each one — before your account is fully removed.

If any organization's subscription cannot be cancelled (for example, a temporary billing system issue), the deletion is paused and you'll be notified rather than leaving an orphaned subscription still being charged.

---

## What Stays vs. What's Deleted

**Permanently deleted on account deletion:**
- Your login credentials and profile
- All properties, bookings, and turnovers you created
- Inventory and purchase order history
- Guidebook configurations and sponsor relationships
- Crew member records and assignment history
- Vendor and work order records

**Not affected by your account deletion:**
- Guests who received SMS messages retain the right to have already-sent message records handled per standard data retention — this is separate from your FieldStay account
- Property owners with an active Owner Portal link will lose access once your account and data are deleted

---

## Your Data and Third Parties

FieldStay does not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data shared with integration partners (OwnerRez, Stripe, Telnyx, Kroger) is limited to what's required for those specific integrations to function, governed by each partner's own data handling terms.
